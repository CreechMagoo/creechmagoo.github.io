NEWS (FEBRUARY 5, 2025)

DOOM: The Gallery Experience v1.2.4

An update to DOOM: The Gallery Experience has been released. The major changes include:

  • Erasing progress will now immediately remove all unlocks where possible
  • Fixed inactivity timer counting up while in menus
  • The game will now visually freeze when processing menu melt effect, preventing jitteriness and slow device oddities
  • Fixed Insightful medal win condition not screaming at you in the results screen
  • Added failsafe for game border not appearing
