NEWS (OCTOBER 7, 2025)

DOOM: The Gallery Experience v1.4.2

An update to DOOM: The Gallery Experience has been released. The major changes include:

  • Added Wasted skin
    • Awarded for drinking everything
    • Wearing this skin may make you a little bit tipsy
  • Shortened the drinking cooldown; drink responsibly
  • Fixed being able to drink during transitions
  • Classic is no longer a secret skin
  • Newgrounds exclusive: Added extra validity checks to the Tankman skin
  • Fixed any drink working for the Sociable medal
  • Adjusted appearance of locked skins and medals
  • Fixed saving not happening automatically when unlocking things on the results screen
  • Prevented saving from happening when it shouldn't
  • Fixed a softlock on the results screen caused by spamming inputs
  • Fixed being able request pointer lock when moving to the results screen
  • Trying to view multiple artworks now prioritizes the closest one
  • Fixed being able to collect pickups from any height
