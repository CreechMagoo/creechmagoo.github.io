NEWS (OCTOBER 18, 2025)

New SCUM DOG game out in December!

We're very happy to announce that SCUM DOG's next game entered development earlier this month. Work has been going steadily, and we're aiming for release in early-to-mid December!

Without getting into too much detail, it's another game as an artform, similar to DOOM: The Gallery Experience. Unlike DOOM: TGE, it's more of an open concept this time around. We're certain many people will still be familiar with what we end up doing.

This will also be our first game to utilize server technology! We found the perfect person to help bring the game to life, and it's been super exciting seeing it come together.

We can't wait for you to get your hands on it, and we'll be seeing you very soon!
