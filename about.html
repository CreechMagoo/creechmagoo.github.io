About Us

SCUM DOG is a group of friends who enjoy working and making games together. We've been working together as a group since early 2020, later going on to publish our first game, Scummoner, in 2024 as a part of Ludum Dare.

After publishing more projects and finding our footing, we've decided to make it official and founded SCUM DOG LTD in early 2025.

We're very excited to keep working on more new and exciting projects!
